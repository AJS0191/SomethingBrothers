About The Something Brothers

We are a Tulsa-based theater company focused on intimate storytelling, bold staging, and productions that feel close enough to touch.

We collaborate with local artists, build shows designed for unique black-box spaces, and keep our process honest, local, and gritty.

Our work is personal, exciting, and always one of a kind.
